How To Use

  1. Turn on Bluetooth on your phone.
  2. Open this page in Chrome on Android over HTTPS.
  3. Power the board and wait for MotionCube BLE.
  4. Tap Connect BLE and choose the device.
  5. Tilt the board and watch the sculpture follow the motion.

Need Attention

No issues.

If Chrome says Bluetooth is unavailable, first check HTTPS, Bluetooth permissions, and whether the board is still advertising.
